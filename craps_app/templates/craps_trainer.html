Betting Guide

Pass Line Bet

  • Wins on a 7 or 11 on the come-out or point pass
  • Loses on 2, 3, or 12 ("craps") only on the come-out roll

Don't Pass Bet

Opposite of pass

Single-Roll Bet Payouts

  • 7: Pays 4:1
  • Craps (2,3,12): Pays 7:1
  • Either 3 or 11: Pays 15:1
  • Either 2 or 12: Pays 15:1
  • Called 2: Pays 30:1
  • Called 12: Pays 30:1

Craps Trainer

How to Play Craps (Basic Rules)

  • Come Out Roll (The First Roll):
  • - Roll 7 or 11: Win! (Natural)
  • - Roll 2, 3, or 12: Lose! (Craps)
  • - Roll 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, or 10: That number becomes the Point.
  • After Point is Established:
  • - Roll the Point again: Win!
  • - Roll 7: Lose! (Seven Out - round ends, new come out roll)
  • - Roll any other number: Roll again.
