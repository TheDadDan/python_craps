How to Play Craps (Basic Rules)

  • **Come Out Roll (First Roll):**
  • - Roll 7 or 11: **Win!** (Natural)
  • - Roll 2, 3, or 12: **Lose!** (Craps)
  • - Roll 4, 5, 6, 8, 9, or 10: That number becomes the **Point**.
  • **After Point is Established:**
  • - Roll the **Point** again: **Win!**
  • - Roll 7: **Lose!** (Seven Out - round ends, new come out roll)
  • - Roll any other number: Roll again.

Press 'Roll Dice' to start a new round!
